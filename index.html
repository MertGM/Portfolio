About me

Ever since I've been a kid, the computer always fascinated me by the possibilities it has.

Communicating anonymously, possibly someone on the other side of the ocean, connected to millions of other people; internet.

Tens of images being shown per second, like a flip book, perceived as one fluid motion; animation/video.

Millions of pixels being manipulated that recreate our world; video games (combining video games and the internet has always been my favorite.)

All inside a computer that consists of billions of transistors; fascinating.
